Welcome to Scuba Diving

by Judy Miston

Mrs.Miston has been diving for 5 years and is really experienced. She takes pride in helping her students learn the ways of diving in the ocean. In scuba diving, we take you out to the Great Barrier Reef for exploring. We teach you about the sea life and for beginner divers, there will always be someone watching making sure you get the best experience.

Hear is some of the scuba diving gear you get to wear and learn about. We teach you the proper way to wear and use scuba gear. We make sure all gear fits each person perfectly with no problems. On average, Scuba Gear costs at least $200 to $300. For more information, click the image above.

Here is also some diving courses that you can take to become perfessional or if you like it just for fun. You can follow many paths and choose the type of diver you want to be. There are many types of diving courses you can be interested in. There is scuba diving, night diving, open-water diving, and many more. To learn more, click the image.

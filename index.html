Snorkling

Here at Araxie, our snorkling classes will teach you the proper way to snorkle. Also, we take you to the coolest coral reefs were the depth is 15ft. We give you the best experience

Scubba Diving

During scubba diving, we take you to the beautiful coral gardens just of our coast. You will see many types of fish, baby sharks, and whales or dolphins.

Swimming with Dolphins

You will get to swim with our bottlenose dolphins. You will learn about all the qualities that make up the dolphins. Swimming with dolphins is a good exprience to bond with the sea and learn about differenct types of dolphins.

LifeGuarding

Here at Lifeguarding, we teach you the ways of safty and how to help children in need. We help you and let you practice lifeguarding by yourselfs. We do have requirments, but most students meet them.
